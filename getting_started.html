Getting Started with Computer Science

What is Computer Science?

Computer Science is the study of computers and computational systems. It involves understanding algorithms, programming languages, and the architecture of computers. It's a field that encompasses both theoretical studies and practical applications.

Brief History

From early computing machines like the ENIAC to modern-day quantum computers, computer science has evolved significantly. Key milestones include the development of algorithms, the creation of programming languages, and the advent of the internet.

Fundamentals of Computer Science

  • Programming Languages: Learn languages like Python, Java, and C++.
  • Data Structures: Understand arrays, linked lists, stacks, queues, and trees.
  • Algorithms: Study sorting, searching, and optimization algorithms.
  • Computer Architecture: Learn how hardware and software interact.
  • Software Engineering: Explore methodologies like Agile and Scrum.

Introductory Project

A good starting project could be building a simple calculator or a to-do list app. This will help you apply basic programming concepts and understand how different parts of a program interact.

Useful Resources

  • Khan Academy: Tutorials on computer science basics.
  • GeeksforGeeks: Articles and coding challenges.
